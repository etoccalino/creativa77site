Meet the team

Julian Cerruti

Julian is the founder of Creativa 77. Throughout his 15+ years of experience he has led many challenging, first-of-their-kind software projects to successful completion.

Despite being a successful manager and businessman, Julian's true passion lies is software development and working with technology in general. In his free time, he likes rollerblading, longboarding, biking and working on side projects such as scratch-building R/C airplanes, quadcopters, 3D printers, and HTPCs.


Tessa Lau

Tessa is a Research Scientist at Willow Garage and a visiting scholar at Creativa 77. She creates simple web-based interfaces for complex personal robots. She knits socks with toes while listening to talks.


Elvio Toccalino

A software fanatic, Elvio has been chewing code for over five years now. Most of his contributions to Creativa 77 have been developing and extending webserver functionality as well as leveraging the newest web technologies to provide real time operation through the browser. His personal interests include physics, math, and music, and he spends his weekends working on side projects.


Sebastián García Marra

Sebastian is a MSEE student at the University of Buenos Aires and has been working at Creativa 77 mostly developing and testing ROS-based systems. He's been a robotics enthusiast since he was a child. He likes software development as well as hardware design and has experience with different embedded platforms. In his spare time, he's currently building a ROS-based robot that will be able to recognize objects, colors and faces. He also works as teaching assistant and takes part in different educational activities in order to spread the word about science.

Lucas Chiesa

He is passionate about learning something new and challenging every day. Lucas is a MSEE from the University of Buenos Aires. His interest in robotics has led him to stablish a highly successful Robotics Club in his university many years ago and more recently to work as a researcher in the AIS lab, Freiburg. His electronic background, expertise in embedded systems (from 8 bit to Embedded Linux) and love for robotics greatly complement his devotion to Open Source software and hardware. Besides his work, he enjoys a good movie, theater, music, traveling, and the occasional side project which every hacker has :)
